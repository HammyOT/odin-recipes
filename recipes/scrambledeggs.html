Scrambled Eggs

Description

Start your day right with this classic scrambled eggs recipe. With just a few ingredients and a couple of minutes, you can enjoy fluffy and flavorful scrambled eggs. Whether it's for a quick weekday breakfast or a leisurely weekend brunch, this recipe is a staple that will never disappoint. Whisked to perfection and cooked until soft and creamy, these scrambled eggs are sure to satisfy your morning cravings. Get ready to savor every delicious bite!

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk the eggs until well beaten. Season with salt and pepper
  2. Heat the butter or oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet and let them cook undisturbed for a few seconds until the edges start to set.
  4. Using a spatula, gently stir and fold the eggs until they form soft curds.
  5. Continue cooking, stirring occasionally, until the eggs are cooked to your desired level of doneness.
  6. Remove from heat and serve the scrambled eggs hot. Enjoy!
