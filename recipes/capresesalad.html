Caprese Salad

Description

Indulge in the vibrant flavors of summer with this fresh Caprese salad. Made with ripe tomatoes, creamy mozzarella cheese, and fragrant basil leaves, this salad is a true celebration of simplicity and quality ingredients. Drizzled with extra virgin olive oil and optionally adorned with a drizzle of balsamic glaze, this salad is a feast for both the eyes and the taste buds. Whether enjoyed as a refreshing appetizer or a light side dish, the combination of flavors in this Caprese salad will transport you to the sunny Mediterranean. Prepare to be delighted by the harmonious marriage of flavors in every bite

Ingredients

  • 2 large ripe tomatoes
  • 8 ounces fresh mozarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Balsamic glaze (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Slice the tomatoes and mozzarella cheese into Â¼-inch thick slices.
  2. Arrange the tomato slices on a serving platter.
  3. Top each tomato slice with a slice of mozzarella cheese.
  4. Place a fresh basil leaf on top of each mozzarella slice.
  5. Drizzle the salad with extra virgin olive oil.
  6. If desired, drizzle balsamic glaze over the salad for added flavor.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve the fresh Caprese salad immediately as a refreshing appetizer or side dish. Enjoy!
